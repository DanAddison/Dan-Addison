About me

Born and raised in Folkestone , I spent 6 years in Kingston-upon-Thames where I studied fine art and developed as a singer-songwriter/ performer. I ended up returning to Folkestone to benefit from, and hopefully contribute to, the town's creative regeneration.

I spent the next few years as a music producer with a day job in a bookshop. I made acoustic stuff , alt hip-hop , and collaborative art rock . I also dabbled in making videos of local cultural highlights for my lo-fi, formless YouTube channel.

In 2016 I decided to move on to new challenges. While living in the Creative Quarter and working night shifts in the local council's control room , I managed to teach myself web development from absolute zero. In 2018 I became a full-time developer, freelancing primarily for local design agency Nebulo.

I love low-angle sunshine, family and friends, indie cinema, languid bike rides, calisthenics, literary fiction, summer sea swimming, Shane Carruth, Alex Honnold, Rose Namajunas, Kendrick Lamar and Alan Partridge.
